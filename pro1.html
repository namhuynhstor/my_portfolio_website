THIRD-PARTY LOGISTICS SOLUTIONS FOR GARMENT SECTOR (Aothun.vn)

Brief about the project

Aothun.vn is the leading clothing and garment manufacturing company in Vietnam. In this case, they face many difficulties in delivering goods to customers.

▪ Aothun.vn has 2 channels for both online and offline sales: one locates in District 1, and one locates in District 12.
▪ The number of online sales is approximately 1000 products/day( 400 in HCM city).
▪ They used Viettel Post Service for transporting to customers, with the price: of 22.000-35.000VND ( accounted for 15-20% value of the product).
▪ Transportation time about 1-2 days rely on the transportation party, 2-3 days rely on other cases of the customer is not present at the point of delivery, and difficult to exchange the products when it is a failure.
